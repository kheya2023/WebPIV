We investigate how physical principles govern biological organisation and function, from the scale of molecules to developing embryos. We ask questions in the context of soft-matter systems and living organisms, our activities covering both fundamental and applied research.

Research Topics

  • Biological membranes: structure, function and interactions
  • Bronchi-on-chip, organoids: self-organization, development, function
  • Cell mechanics and mechanotransduction
  • Microcirculation of blood cells
  • Nanosensors for biology and health
  • Theory of soft and living matter
